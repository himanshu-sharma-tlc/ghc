We welcome you to the 9th Global Hospitality Conclave. Participation is open to OSHM / OCLD graduates from 1968 till 2019 and those who currently hold or have previously held executive roles at The Oberoi Group for a period of more than five years.


Gain valuable global insights from foreign policy expert and our Keynote Speaker Prof. C Raja Mohan. Learn about the latest in the hospitality business from Rajeev Menon, President APEC, Marriott International in dialogue with Oberoi alumni Professor Chekitan Dev of Cornell University.


Relive past memories and Immerse yourself in a session of anecdotes and tales in a fireside chat with SS Mukherji, former Vice Chairman and Managing Director EIH Ltd. We have a panel on Oberoi success stories consisting of Mohit Nirula, COO of The Oberoi Group in conversation with Arif Patel, CCO ITC Hotels and Vivek Shukla CEO of The Lalit Hotels.


We are finalizing the remaining sessions and the detailed agenda for the day will be updated on our website. We will end with a session on Conscious Leadership by Oberoi alumni S Ranganath.


The Registration fee for full day attendance is Rs. 6000 including taxes. Delegates may invite their for the evening event at Rs. 3500 including taxes. Special rates for accommodation are available here.


You may contact any member of the Organizing Committee for further information. We look forward to seeing you.
